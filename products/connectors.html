What are Connectors?

Connectors are electromechanical devices that join electrical circuits together, enabling the transmission of power and signals between different components, boards, or systems. They provide a removable interface that allows for easy assembly, maintenance, and reconfiguration of electronic systems. Connectors are essential for creating modular, serviceable, and scalable electronic designs.

Think of connectors like the plugs and sockets in your home - they allow you to easily connect and disconnect electrical devices without permanent wiring. In electronics, connectors serve the same purpose but are designed for specific applications, from tiny micro-connectors in smartphones to robust industrial connectors that can withstand harsh environments.
